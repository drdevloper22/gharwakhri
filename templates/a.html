About Gharwakhari

Gharwakhari is a trial e-commerce website created for learning and practice. This platform showcases different categories such as home décor, gift items, and lifestyle products with a clean and modern design.

Our goal is to understand front-end design, layout structuring, and user experience using HTML and CSS.
